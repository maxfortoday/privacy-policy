Quick Summary (Plain English)
  • No signup required. We don't ask for your email, name, age, or anything personal.
  • We don't sell your data. Period.

This local data can be cleared anytime by uninstalling the app or clearing app data through your device settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Operate the app — maintain your anonymous account, save your scores
  • Improve the app — understand which genres are popular, where users drop off, what's broken
  • Fix bugs — diagnose crashes through Crashlytics stack traces
  • Show ads — Google AdMob serves banner and rewarded video ads to keep the app free
  • Communicate with you — only if you email us first

We do not use your data for:

  • Selling to third parties
  • Building marketing profiles for resale
  • Cross-app tracking outside our own analytics
  • Behavioral targeting beyond what AdMob provides for ad serving

5. Advertising and Tracking Choices

5.1 iOS App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS 14.5 and later, when you first open the app you'll see a system prompt asking permission to track your device's advertising identifier. If you decline, AdMob shows non-personalized ads (still functional, just not tailored).

5.2 Limit ad tracking

You can reset or disable your advertising identifier at any time:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → toggle off, or Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → Personalized Ads off
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization (or "Delete advertising ID")

5.3 Disable analytics in-app

You can disable in-app analytics events anytime in Settings → Privacy → Analytics (within TrackID).

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your data.

7.1 Everyone

You can:

  • Stop using the app at any time by uninstalling it. This wipes local data immediately.
  • Request deletion of your anonymous server-side records by emailing admtrigger@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my data" and including the anonymous user ID shown in Settings → About.
  • Limit ads through your device's advertising ID controls (see Section 5).

7.2 EU / UK residents (GDPR)

You have the right to: access, rectify, erase, restrict, and port your data, object to processing, and lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority.

Email admtrigger@gmail.com with "GDPR Request" in the subject line. We respond within 30 days.

Legal basis for processing under GDPR:

  • Anonymous account + game progress: legitimate interest in providing the service
  • Analytics: legitimate interest in improving the app
  • Advertising: consent (via ATT prompt on iOS, opt-out on Android)
  • Crash reports: legitimate interest in app stability

7.3 California residents (CCPA / CPRA)

We do not sell your personal information. AdMob's ad serving may be considered "sharing" under CPRA — you can opt out via the iOS ATT prompt or Android ad ID controls.

7.4 Israeli residents (PPL)

You have the right to access, correct, or delete personal information about you, per Israel's Privacy Protection Law 5741-1981 (חוק הגנת הפרטיות). Email admtrigger@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

8. Children's Privacy

TrackID is rated 12+ on the App Store and Teen on Google Play. The app is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in the EU/UK).

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the applicable age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child under the applicable age has provided personal information to us, please contact us at admtrigger@gmail.com and we will promptly delete the data.

The 12+ rating reflects that some music tracks streamed from Deezer's catalog may contain explicit lyrics. We do not control individual track content.

9. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information we collect:

  • All network traffic uses HTTPS / TLS encryption
  • Firestore data is access-restricted by Firebase Security Rules — users can only read/write their own data
  • We do not store passwords (no signup = no passwords)
  • Cloud infrastructure (Firebase, AdMob) is operated by Google with industry-standard security

No system is 100% secure. If we become aware of a security incident affecting your data, we will notify you and the relevant authorities as required by applicable law.
